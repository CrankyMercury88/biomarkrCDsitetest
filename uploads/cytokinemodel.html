What you're looking at

The Q-SENS platform measures a cytokine panel from a finger-prick blood sample in under 10 minutes.

This simulator models how those panel readings change across disease states, patient profiles, and time — using biologically-informed modeled trajectories derived from published immunopathology literature.

Select a condition below, adjust patient variables, and move the time slider to explore the immune signatures Q-SENS is designed to detect.
